Sailor Moon

For love and justice, I'm the pretty soldier Sailor Moon. In place of the moon, I'll punish you!

My name is Tsukino Usagi. I'm 14 years old, and in second year of junior high. My birthday is June 30th, and my birthstone is a pearl. My sign is Cancer, and my blood type is O. My character is a little more careless than other people, and a little bit of a cry baby. One day a strange black cat Luna appeared, gave me a strange transformation brooch, and made me a sailor suit fighter. She told me to fight against bad ones, but I'm very uncertain.

I am the fighter for justice, Sailor Moon. I can also transform into Super Sailor Moon, and Eternal Sailor Moon.

My junior high is Juuban Junior High School in Minato-ku. I am in second year, class 1. My favorite subject in school is home economics, and I hate math and English. My favorite foods are ice cream and cake. My least favorite food is carrots. My favorite colors are pink and white. My hobbies are eating and sleeping. I currently live with my family (father, mother, younger brother) in a house in Minato-ku.

Sailor Mars

In the name of Mars, I'll punish you!

My name is Hino Rei, please call me Rei-chan. I'm 14 years old, and in second year of junior high. My birthday is April 17th. My sign is Aries, and my blood type is AB. I am also the fighter for justice, Sailor Mars.

My junior high is T*A Private Girls' School. My favorite subject in school is ancient literature, and my least favorite subject is modern social studies. I am considered a "queen" at school, and the other students are happy to wait on me.

My favorite foods are fugu (puffer fish) and thai food. My least favorite food is canned asparagus. But I will eat a lot of everything. My favorite colors are red and black. My future dream is to be a singer/song writer, a model, a wonderful seiyuu, and a wife.

I currently live with my grandfather at Hikawa Jinja (a shrine).

Sailor Mercury

Douse yourself in some water and repent!

My name is Mizuno Ami. I'm 14 years old, and in second year of junior high. My birthday is September 10th. My sign is Virgo, and my blood type is A. I am also the fighter for justice, Sailor Mercury.

My junior high is Juuban Junior High School in Minato-ku. I am in second year, class 5. My favorite subject in school is mathematics, and there isn't any subject I dislike. Someone said that my IQ was 300.

My favorite foods are sandwiches and anmitsu. My least favorite food is yellow-tail tuna. My favorite color is light blue. I like reading and playing chess. My favorite sport is swimming. As Sailor Mercury I use a mini super computer to find out information about the enemy.

I currently live with my mother, who is a doctor, in a mansion in Minato-ku.

Sailor Jupiter

Protected by Jupiter, the planet of thunder, Guardian of Protection, Sailor Jupiter!

My name is Kino Makoto. Everyone calls me Mako. I'm 14 years old, and in second year of junior high. My birthday is December 5th. My sign is Saggitarius, and my blood type is O. I am also the fighter for justice, Sailor Jupiter.

My junior high is Juuban Junior High School in Minato-ku. I am in second year, class 6. My favorite subject in school is home economics, and my least favorite is physics. My favorite foods are cherry pie and meat loaf. There isn't any food that I dislike. I like cooking very much. My favorite color is sugar pink. My hobby is cooking.

Sailor Venus

Protected by Venus, the planet of beauty, Guardian of Love, Sailor Venus! Allow me to punish you with the power of love!

My name is Aino Minako. I'm 14 years old, and in second year of junior high. My birthday is October 22. My sign is Libra, and my blood type is B. I am also the fighter for justice, Sailor Venus.

I also used to go around as Sailor V before joining the other Sailor Senshi.

My junior high is Shibakouen Junior High School in Minato-ku. My favorite subject in school is physical education, and my least favorite is everything else. I used to be on the volleyball team before I went to England.

My favorite foods are gyouza, ramen, and curry rice. I don't like shiitake mushrooms. My favorite colors are red and yellow. My hobby is just wasting time. I currently live with my family (father and mother).
